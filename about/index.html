About me

Hi! I'm Gabriela. My friends call me Gabs or Gaby. I started The Unapologetic Blog - TUB 🛁, as an opportunity to share the things that I learned over time that I find interesting (I like to take notes of everything 📝) and I would like to share with others. The knowledge becomes more significant when you share it. The main topic discussed here is technology, but I would also like to write about other areas of my life as I discover them along the way. I hope you enjoy going with me in this journey.

It's unapologetic because I want to feel I can write about anything without having to apologize because it's not too technical, because it's not in the original scope, because the posts are too long or too short 🫠. Just to write accurate free content it's enough for the moment.

I'm a Colombian Chemical Engineer 🧪 living in Montreal 🍁, who decided (as many nowadays) to try programming, which is what I have been doing since 2020. I have just finished an AEC in Programming and Internet Technologies at College Universel, so a great part of this blog it's composed by my class notes. I'm also a researcher and science teacher 👩‍🔬. In my free time, I am a meditation instructor 🧘‍♀️ and I play ukulele 🎵.

You will realize that I can be as diversified in the number of things I like to learn, as the colors of the rainbow 🌈. And I think that is the way all human beings are💃. It's hard (and boring) to frame people in only one area of their lives.
